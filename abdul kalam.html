A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th president of india form 2002 to 2007. Born and rised in a muslim family in Rameswaram he studied Physics and aerospace enjineering he spent the next four decades as a scientist and Science Administrator mainly at theDefence Research and Devolopment Organization and was intimatley involved in indians civilianspace program and military missile devolopment efforts he thus came to be known as the Missile Man of India for his work on the devolopment of Ballistic Missile and launch vehical technology. he also played a pivotal organizational, technical, and political role in in indias Pokhran-11 nuclear test in 1998
